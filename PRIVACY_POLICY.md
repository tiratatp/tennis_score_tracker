# Privacy Policy for Tennis Score Tracker

**Effective Date:** March 18, 2026
**Developer:** NuttyKnot

## Introduction

Tennis Score Tracker is a tennis scoring app for Android and Wear OS. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Tennis Score Tracker does **not** collect any personal information. All data — including match scores, player names, and settings — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

## Internet & Network Access

The app currently does not require or use internet access. All data stays on your device(s). Phone-to-watch communication uses the Wearable Data Layer (local Bluetooth/Wi-Fi Direct), not the internet. Future versions may introduce internet access solely for features that benefit you, such as cloud sync or enhanced analysis — see "Future Updates" below.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The app is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since no personal information is collected from any user, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If future versions introduce data collection, parental consent will be obtained before collecting data from children under 13.

## Future Updates

Future versions may collect data such as match statistics or health metrics (e.g., heart rate) to enhance the user experience. Such data may be stored on your device(s) or on secure cloud servers solely for purposes that directly benefit you (e.g., syncing across devices, providing enhanced statistics or analysis). If cloud features are introduced, internet access will be used exclusively for these user-facing features. Your data will **never be sold, shared with, or disclosed to any third-party companies or services**. This policy will be updated before any such changes take effect.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs integrated into the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
